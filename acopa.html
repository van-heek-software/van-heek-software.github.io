ACOPA-Patrol

Alberta Citizens On Patrol Association (A.C.O.P.A.) was formed to assist Citizens On Patrol (C.O.P.) groups throughout Alberta through support, development, and sustainability of C.O.P. programs. The ACOPA-Patrol App is designed to assist and record the activities of C.O.P. on patrol in their communities.
Users can document community interactions, patrol routes, and various activities by taking photos and filling out short forms. The location of many different types of incidences will be recorded and displayed on an interactive map as well as summarized into a report (consisting of both a PDF and an Excel document). The report can then be emailed to a designated department or specific individuals to advise them of the documented issues.
